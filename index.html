Introduction

LookBench is a comprehensive fashion image retrieval benchmark featuring:

  • Four Diverse Subtasks:
    • AI-Gen StreetLook: AI-generated street fashion scenarios
    • Real StreetLook: Real-world street fashion images
    • AI-Gen Studio: AI-generated studio fashion photography
    • Real Studio: Professional studio fashion photography
  • Three Key Metrics:
    • Coarse Recall: Measures broad retrieval accuracy
    • Fine Recall: Evaluates precise matching capability
    • nDCG: Normalized Discounted Cumulative Gain for ranking quality
  • Multiple Evaluation Points: @1, @5, @10, and @20 for comprehensive performance assessment
